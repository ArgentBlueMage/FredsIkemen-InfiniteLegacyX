Profile:
=============
-Master Huang
=============
Specials Skills: All Kind of Martial Art, Own Creation: Shadowless Kick
Age : 24
Sex: Male
Personality: Righteous, Honorable
Hobby: None
Relative: Father-Huang, Chi Yin

 

His Father and him were known as the "Ten Tigers of Canton",
Family Secret "Shadowless Kick" was well-known (some secret). The
Institution "Po Chi Lin" was created to heal and help people. His
friend Dragon was kidnapped, and he helped Crane in a rescue mission.
He was an Expert in Shaolin Kung Fu, and developed new techniques like
"Ten Words Fist" and Shadowless Kick. In the game he belongs to the
most powerful character. He can combine different skills and
techniques during the fight. Strategic Attacks are the main uses of
this character.

 

Moves

Hard Punch

• Command: +x or y.

•Description: Master Huang dash forward and does a very powerful punch, it can make the foe fall if connects.

 

Straight Kick

• Command: +a or b.

• Description: Does a fast kick on the enemy. If connect, then you can press + a or b for an add-on.

 

Ascending Dragon

• Command: +a or b.

• Description: Does a anti-air attack. If connect, then you can press + a or b for an add-on.

 

Consequence Kick

• Command: +a or b.

• Description: Does two fast kicks, if the 2nd kick connect then you can do a button mash with a or b.
 

Swap Kick

• Command: +a or b.

• Description: Master Huang does a flying kick in the enemy if the move connect then it can be combined with +a or b for more hits.

 

Shadow Less Kick

• Command: +a or b (in air).

• Description: Does fast kicks in the enemy before landing. If the enemy defend, then Master Huang will escape from a counter attack.

 

 

Special Throw

 

•Command: +y

• Description: Can change positions with the enemy and making him stunned for few time.

 

Super Motions:

 

Buddah Strike

• Command: +x or y (consume one power bar).

• Description: Does a very powerful chi move which can hit eight times plus sending the foe far away.

 

The Wind Strike

• Command: +a or b (consume one power bar).

• Description: Does a flying kick, if it hits (and the enemy not defend) then Master Huang will do a flurry of attacks hitting the foe 20 times (18 in the original).
