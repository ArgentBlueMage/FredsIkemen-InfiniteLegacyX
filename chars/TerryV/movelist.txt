                                    Terry XIII by Vyn

--FEATURES--

Perfect Guard
Recovery Roll (hold back or fwd while on the ground)
Hold Back while jumping: Air Guard
HP+HK While Guarding: Zero Counter

--CANCELLING--

Normal attacks into stronger normal attacks
Special moves into special moves
Special Moves into Supers
Supers into other equal or stronger supers

BACK+HP: Launcher
FWD+MK: Overhead

--SYSTEM--

Neutral PP: Forward Throw
Neutral KK: Back throw
PPP or KKK: Power charge
LP+LK: Evade
MP+MK: Ex-Skill
HP+HK: Ex-Trigger

--SPECIAL MOVES--

QCF+P: Powerwave (Air)
QCB+P: Burn knuckle (Air)
QCF+K: Rising tackle
QCB+K: Crack shoot (Air)

--SUPERS--

QCF+PP: Power geyser
QDB+PP: Buster wolf 
QCF+KK: Power dunk (lvl2)
QCF,QCF+P: Trinity Geyser (lvl3)
QCF,QCF+K: Legendary Hungry Wolf (lvl5)
