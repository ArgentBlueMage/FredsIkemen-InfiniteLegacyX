Special Attacks 
-----------------------
Shadow Attack 1 (takes 500 power) : HCB + a

Shadow Attack 2 (takes 500 power) : HCB + b

Heart Strike : QCB + x or y
-->Heart Smash (exactly when Heart Strike hits) : F + x or y

EX Heart Strike (takes 500 power) : QCB + x+y
    
Hill Mover : QCB + a or b

Trough Lock : DP + a or b

T.Hold : QCF + a/b/x/y

Monk Grab : HCB, F + x or y
     
Super Attacks (uses 1 power bar )
-------------
Monk Seal : 2QCF + x or y

T.Fall : 2QCF + a or b

Hyper Attacks (uses 2 power bars)
-------------
Monk Slam : 2HCB + x+y
